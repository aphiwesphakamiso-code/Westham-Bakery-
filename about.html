About Westham Bakery

From a small community bakery to a Durban institution — discover the heart behind every loaf and cake.

Who We Are

Baked with Heart in Sydenham

Westham Bakery has been a cornerstone of the Sydenham Berea community in Durban. Located at 588 Randles Road, we have been dedicated to bringing joy to every table through freshly baked goods made with the finest ingredients.

What started as a passion for baking has grown into a beloved local institution. Our team of dedicated bakers, decorators, and front-of-house staff work tirelessly — around the clock — to ensure that whether you walk in at midday or midnight, you're greeted with freshness and warmth.

We believe in three things above all: quality ingredients, exceptional service, and the kind of freshness that can only come from baking every single day. Our customers aren't just customers — they're community, family, and friends.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every decision we make comes back to these principles.

Quality First

We source only the finest flour, dairy, and fresh produce. No shortcuts — ever. Every product that leaves our kitchen meets our high standards before it reaches you.

Community Love

We're not just a bakery — we're a part of Sydenham. We celebrate your birthdays, comfort your Sundays, and fuel your mornings. The community is our inspiration.

Hygiene & Freshness

Our kitchen maintains the highest standards of cleanliness. Our customers consistently praise our hygiene, and we take that responsibility seriously every single day.

Exceptional Service

Our staff are trained to assist, advise, and go the extra mile — like decorating your birthday cake at no extra charge because your smile is reward enough.

Always Open

Life doesn't stop at 5pm, and neither do we. Operating 24 hours means you always have a warm, fresh option available — no matter the hour.

Creative Craftsmanship

From custom-designed celebration cakes to artisan sourdough, we bring artistry to everything we bake. Food should be beautiful as much as it is delicious.

Our Journey

Milestones & Moments

The Beginning

Westham Bakery Opens

Westham Bakery opens its doors at 588 Randles Road, Sydenham, with a vision to bring quality baking to the Durban community.

Growth

24-Hour Trading Introduced

Responding to growing demand from the community, Westham introduces round-the-clock trading — cementing its place as Durban's most reliable bakery.

Expansion

Custom Cake Studio Launched

We launch our custom cake ordering service, allowing customers to commission bespoke celebration cakes for every occasion.

Today

173 Reviews & Still Growing

With over 173 glowing Google reviews and a 4.3-star rating, Westham Bakery continues to serve Durban with pride, passion, and freshness every day.
